Who We Are

I was born and raised in Lomé, Togo (West Africa) where I graduated High School in September 2012. I have attended The University of Lome where I completed an Associate Degree in 2015. Seeking for better opportunities and reuniting with my family I immigrated to Philadelphia, Pennsylvania in 2016. I then enlisted in the United States Army and graduated Basic Combat Training in June 2020 at Fort Leonard Wood, Missouri.

What We Have Accomplished

August 2020, I received orders to my first duty station Fort Hood, TX and was assigned to Alpha Battery, 1st Battalion 62nd Air Defense Artillery Regiment. In September 2020, I deployed to Saudi Arabia in support of Operation Enduring Freedom. During this year tour I served as the battery’s CBRN Specialist and Orderly Room Clerk. I assisted the battery with proper CBRN training, accountability of CBRN equipment, I processed over 200 HR actions, to include emergency leaves, pay inquiry, post deployment leave for the entire unit, and over 20 Basic Leaders Course packets. Upon completion of this tour, I received two Army Achievement Medals, one Overseas Service Ribbon and one Army Commendation Medal.
